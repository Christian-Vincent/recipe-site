Pasta Va Zool Recipe

A warm, filling dish is always welcome during the winter. This one will not only do both, but make you ask for seconds! Combining sweet hot sausage, a variety of spices, and a little bit of broth, this easy to make pasta dish will leave you and anyone else eating it begging for more!

    Ingredients
  • 1x Bowtie Pasta
  • Hot Sausage (1lb.)
  • 1x Drained Can of White Beans
  • 1x Chicken Boulion Cube
  • 1 Tablespoon Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Tablespoon Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 2 Tablespoons Salt
  • 1 cup Boiling Water

Instructions

Step 1

In a medium pot, bring six cups of water to a boil. Once the water has boiled, add salt and your pasta. Follow the time given by your pasta box to reach al dente. Drain pasta with a strainer once completed. Return to pot.

Step 2

While the water boils, heat a tablespoon of vegetable oil to in a medium saucepan for a minute. Afterwards, add your sausage by breaking it up into tiny chunks. Stir in the saucepan until browned.

Step 3

Add your garlic powder and red pepper flakes. Let them soak for 1 minute.

Step 4

Toss drained beans in. Stir to mix with sausage. Let them sit for 3 minutes.

Step 5

Use a laddle to scoop out a cup of boiling water. Put boulion cube inside. Break it apart before adding to saucepan. Let it sit for 5 minutes.

Step 6

Dump contents of saucepan into pot. Mix with pasta and enjoy!

Well done!

Substitutes

For those of you who have dietary restrictions, there are a few workarounds for this...

  1. Vegan Sausage instead of Hot Sausage
  2. Vegetable Broth instead of Chicken Broth
